Artist Statement

My work is a variety of things ranging from understanding the basics of web design to creating something. My sites are made in HTML and CSS while the Adobe creative suite is used to make my videos and gifs. Blender is where I do my three dimensional modeling used in some of my work. An idea or theme is where I start when using creating and I like to let my work flow from there. My work is inspired from both pop culture and people like Stuart Regan who emphazizes simplicty in his web design. I wish to make websites that are simplistic and effective in their design while allowing my other works to be more creative and in-depth.

For my sites I tend to choose more neutral colors in my websites as I think it allows for a more approachable UI. When using imagery in my sites I like for it to only be things that are important to the information on the page. When I create things like three dimensional models, videos, and gifs I use more abstract colors to help guide the viewer in the direction I want for the piece.
