T.V. World

This is t.v. world, a short animation I made using blender and premiere. My idea was to try and create a world set inside of a televison which is why alot of the colors are vibrant. I kept the main characters limbs darker to help him contrast with his surroundings better. I wanted to make it feel like the television controlled and influenced the environment has different t.v.'s in it. However I also wanted to add something that let you know it was still a living place so I decided to use this tutorial to help make the trees you see in the backround. I tried to go for a strange and mysterious tone like an episode of the twilight zone which is why I used eerie ambient music to help set the tone.
